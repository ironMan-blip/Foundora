How Foundora works

From profile to funding, in one flow

Foundora is built around a simple loop: create a strong profile, discover the right matches, start a conversation, and track progress until the deal is closed. Below is the full journey for both startups and investors.

For startups

  1. 1) Create your startup profile
    Share your stage, traction summary, what you are building, and how much you want to raise. A complete profile builds trust.
  2. 2) Search investors
    Find investors by name, and filter by investing amount range to match your round size.
  3. 3) Message with context
    Start a conversation, send quick updates, and use emoji reactions in chat to keep it human.
  4. 4) Track your pipeline
    Use the funding tracker stages (Intro, Meeting, Due Diligence, Committed, Closed) so you always know what is next.
  5. 5) Schedule meetings
    Pick a time, agenda, and meeting link. Foundora keeps the meeting list organized in one place.

For investors

  1. 1) Create your investor profile
    Define your ticket size, focus areas, and geography. Better inputs means better matches.
  2. 2) Search startups
    Search any startup by name, then open profiles to understand the story quickly.
  3. 3) Shortlist and message
    Keep conversations tidy, delete chats that are no longer relevant, and request a deck when ready.
  4. 4) Manage deal progress
    Move deals through stages, set the next step, and keep an internal view of your pipeline.
  5. 5) Meet and follow up
    Schedule meetings and get reminders through notifications so nothing slips.

Search and filter

Investors can search startups by name. Startups can search investors by name and filter by investing amount range. This keeps discovery fast and relevant.

Messaging

Conversations are built for speed: quick replies, emoji picker like modern messengers, and a delete chat action when you want to clean your inbox.

Funding tracker

A modern pipeline view with stage filters, progress indicators, and next-step notes. Foundora makes fundraising feel manageable.

Ready to try it?

Join Foundora from the landing page. Create your account, complete your profile, then explore search, chat, meetings, notifications, and the funding tracker.
